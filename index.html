History of Brands




Gaud Trade is a very young factory that in less than fifteen years has reached a top market position with a very casual and young fashion. Their jeans, T-Shirt and sweatshirts are on common language spoken worldwide. Their garments are an all-purpose, comfortable fashion perfectly suitable for every occasion from disco and leisure time to formal meeting and office time. At present Gaud Trade is an established company whose main advantages are fashion, research and style. The company key to successful business has been the capture of elements of continuity within the indefinite and ever changing teenagers mood and the making of best-sellers out of them for a weel planned insdustrial production.

Denny Rose is an Italian fashion house founded in Carpi, near Modena. The brand is dedicated only for young girls. The style is very personal and Denny Rose offers modern and sexy fashion at an affordable price. The Denny Rose brand is currently dedicated in three lines: Denny Rose, the main line, Denny Rose Lady, the more elegant and sophisticated line, and Denny Rose Young Girl, which is for children and teenagers. Furthermore Denny Rose also launched a line of knitwear, which is much appreciated. The knitwear is made of lambswool or angora.
